Pasta Bolognese

Penne Pasta with Beef Bolognese

Pasta bolognese is a very quick and simple disk to make, the most simple version can be made using just 3 items from your fridge and pantry! it's great for a quick filling snack that can fill the stomach, or make a large batch of it to have meals ready to be eaten for the rest of the week.

    Ingredients

  • Penne Pasta
  • Beef Mince
  • Jar of Bolognese sauce
  • salt
  • parmesan cheese(optional)
  • garnish(optional)

Cooking steps

  1. Bring a pan of water to the boil
  2. add in the pasta (and a pinch of salt to stop the pasta sticking) and let simmer for 7 minutes
  3. While the pasta is cooking, heat a teaspoon of oil into a frying pan and fry your mince (season if you like), once cooked set aside until pasta is cooked
  4. After frying the mince, put the bolognese sauce in a pan and cook, stirring occasionally until hot
  5. Once pasta is cooked, strain the pasta to remove the water, then spoon the pasta onto a plate or into a bowl
  6. take your mince and put it into to pan of bolognese sauce, stir it to mix it in and spoon onto your pasta
  7. Garnish with a little parmesan cheese or some herbs to add to the flavour
  8. Enjoy your meal!
